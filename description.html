About the Home

The landscape design for the Limestone trails housing development highlights the natural beauty of the Texas Hill Country with native wildflowers, heritage oaks, and sustainable xeriscaping. Limestone paths wind through drought-tolerant gardens, leading to shaded picnic areas, fire pits, and splash pads. Rain gardens and dry creek beds enhance stormwater management, while scenic lookouts and gazebos provide stunning Hill Country views, creating a harmonious, eco-friendly community space.
